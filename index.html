That's Nice.

Your personal wine buyer.

So you like good wine, but find it hard to choose when faced with rows of bottles in the supermarket aisle and countless wine newsletters in your email inbox. Do you go with the shiny medals, or what’s on special that week?

Let me help. I’m here to take the hassle and disappointment out of finding wine that you like, at the best price – and I’ll arrange for it to be delivered promptly to your door.

How does it work?

  1. Let me know what you like/don’t like, and what your budget is, by answering a few questions via email.
  2. Based on your preferences and budget, I will then scout the best offers online, and put together a box of mixed wines for you.
  3. I will let you know the cost of your order, including freight and my fee, and you pay by direct credit.
  4. Once I receive payment, your wine will arrive at your home or work within a week.
  5. Get back in touch with me when you need a top up.

What does it cost?

Minimum per bottle budget is $15.

Minimum order is six bottles.

Service fee: $20 per order.

Service includes:

Personally selecting wines based on your preferences.

Negotiating the best price possible, minimising any freight charges when I can.

There’s a lot of great wine out there, and some good deals – I will find them for you!
